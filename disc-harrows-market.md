<p><p>Disc harrows are essential tillage tools used in agriculture, categorized into three market types: Single Action, Offset Type, and Double Action. Single Action harrows consist of discs mounted on a single frame that operate in one direction, efficiently breaking up soil. Offset Type harrows feature an offset design allowing better maneuverability and coverage. Double Action harrows have discs arranged for simultaneous forward and backward movement, enhancing soil mixing and leveling. Each type serves specific needs in land preparation and soil management.</p></p>

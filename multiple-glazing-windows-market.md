<p><p>The multiple glazing windows market includes various types tailored for energy efficiency and performance. Reflective coatings deflect solar radiation, reducing cooling costs. Spectrally selective coatings filter specific wavelengths, optimizing light while blocking heat. Other types may include laminated or tempered glass, providing additional safety and soundproofing. Each type addresses different energy efficiency needs and climate conditions.</p></p>

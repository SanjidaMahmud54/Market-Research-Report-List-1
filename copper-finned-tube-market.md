<p><p>The Copper Finned Tube market includes Single Metal Finned Tubes and Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes. Single Metal Finned Tubes are made entirely of copper, offering excellent thermal conductivity and corrosion resistance, ideal for high-efficiency heat transfer applications. Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes combine copper with another metal, such as aluminum or steel, balancing cost-effectiveness with enhanced durability and heat dissipation. Both types are widely used in HVAC, refrigeration, and industrial heating systems, catering to diverse thermal management needs.</p></p>

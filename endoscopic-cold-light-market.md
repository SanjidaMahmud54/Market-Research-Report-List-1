<p><p>The Endoscopic Cold Light Market comprises three primary types: Halogen Lamps, Optical Fiber Cold Light Sources, and Xenon Lamps. Halogen lamps offer a cost-effective option with decent light output, but they may generate heat. Optical Fiber Cold Light Sources provide efficient light transmission without heat, enhancing patient comfort. Xenon Lamps deliver high-intensity, bright white light, ideal for detailed imaging, but are generally more expensive. Each type serves specific needs in medical procedures, impacting the choice based on quality, cost, and application.</p></p>

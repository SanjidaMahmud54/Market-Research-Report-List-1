<p><ul><li>Dry Clutch</li><li>Wet Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile clutch market is primarily divided into two types: dry clutch and wet clutch. A dry clutch operates in a separate environment from the engine oil, typically offering direct engagement and efficient power transfer, making it popular in manual transmissions. In contrast, a wet clutch is immersed in engine oil, providing smoother engagement and better heat dissipation, which enhances performance and longevity. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing vehicle design and operational efficiency in various automotive sectors.</p></p>

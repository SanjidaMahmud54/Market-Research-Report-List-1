<p><p>The palm stearin market is divided into two main types: soft stearin and hard stearin. Soft stearin has a lower melting point, making it suitable for applications like margarine and spreads where a softer texture is desired. In contrast, hard stearin has a higher melting point and is more stable, commonly used in baking, confectionery, and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing texture and stability in food products and other industries.</p></p>

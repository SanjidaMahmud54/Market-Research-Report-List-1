<p><p>The scientific lasers market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Solid State Lasers** utilize a solid gain medium, offering high output power and efficiency, ideal for materials processing and medical applications. </p><p>2. **Gas Lasers** employ gas mixtures as the gain medium, known for stability and precision, commonly used in cutting and engraving. </p><p>3. **Liquid Lasers** operate with dye solutions, providing tunable wavelengths, suitable for scientific research. </p><p>4. **Semiconductor Lasers** are compact and energy-efficient, frequently used in telecommunications and consumer electronics. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>

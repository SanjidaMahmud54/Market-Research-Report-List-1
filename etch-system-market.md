<p><p>The Etch System market encompasses various technologies essential for semiconductor manufacturing. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) utilizes electromagnetic fields to create plasma for precise etching. Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) combines chemical and physical processes for enhanced material removal and control over feature sizes. Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) allows for deep, vertical etches, crucial for MEMS and nanotechnology applications. Other methods may include specialized etching techniques tailored for specific materials or applications, broadening the market's scope and versatility.</p></p>

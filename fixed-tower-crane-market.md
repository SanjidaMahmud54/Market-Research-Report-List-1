<p><p>The fixed tower crane market primarily features two main types: horizontal arm and movable arm cranes. Horizontal arm cranes, characterized by a stable vertical tower and a fixed horizontal jib, offer high lifting capacity and stability, making them ideal for large construction sites. In contrast, movable arm cranes have a rotating arm that enhances flexibility and maneuverability, allowing for operation in tighter spaces. Each type serves distinct project requirements, influencing their demand and market dynamics within the construction industry.</p></p>

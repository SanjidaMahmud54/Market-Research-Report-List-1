<p><p>Nepheline market types include Nepheline Syenite, Nepheline Monzonite, and Nephelinites, each serving distinct applications. Nepheline Syenite, a natural glass-ceramic, is primarily used in ceramics and glass production due to its low thermal expansion. Nepheline Monzonite, with balanced quartz and nepheline, is favored in construction and ornamental stone. Nephelinites, rich in nepheline and low in silica, are utilized in specialized industries like refractories and abrasives, highlighting their versatility in modern manufacturing and construction sectors.</p></p>

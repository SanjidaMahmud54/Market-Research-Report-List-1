<p><p>The wafer dicing blade market comprises two primary types: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades feature a central hub that facilitates mounting on cutting equipment, offering stability and versatility for various materials. In contrast, hubless dicing blades lack a central hub, enabling a lighter design and reduced friction, which is ideal for precise cutting applications. Both types cater to different production needs in semiconductor and electronics industries, optimizing efficiency and accuracy in wafer dicing processes.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Electronic Metal Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic metal detectors market consists of three primary types: Very Low Frequency (VLF), Pulse Induction (PI), and Beat-Frequency Oscillation (BFO). VLF detectors operate at low frequencies, making them effective for locating smaller metal objects, commonly used in treasure hunting. PI detectors send short pulses, enabling deep penetration into the ground, ideal for gold prospecting and mineral detection. BFO detectors utilize two oscillators to detect metals, offering simplicity and affordability, typically used for general metal detection tasks.</p></p>

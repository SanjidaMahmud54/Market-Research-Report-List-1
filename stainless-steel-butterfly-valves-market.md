Electric butterfly valves use an electric actuator for automated control, ideal for large-scale systems requiring precise operation and remote management. The "others" category encompasses pneumatic and hydraulic actuated valves, providing additional options for specific industrial needs, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in various fluid control applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The 3D Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) system market is categorized into Off-line and In-line systems. Off-line SPI systems operate separately from the production line, allowing for batch inspections of solder paste quality before components are placed. In contrast, In-line SPI systems are integrated into the manufacturing process, providing real-time monitoring and immediate feedback on solder paste application during production. Both types enhance quality control, reduce defects, and optimize overall manufacturing efficiency in electronics assembly.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Direct System</li><li>Indirect System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Grease Lube System market comprises two primary types: direct and indirect systems. Direct systems deliver grease directly to the lubrication points, ensuring precise application and minimizing waste. They are typically used in high-demand applications. Indirect systems, on the other hand, distribute grease through a network of hoses or tubes to multiple lubrication points, allowing for a more versatile approach to maintenance. Both systems enhance equipment reliability and efficiency, catering to various industrial and automotive needs.</p></p>

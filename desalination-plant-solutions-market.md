<p><p>The desalination plant solutions market includes various technologies to convert seawater or brackish water into potable water. Reverse osmosis utilizes a membrane to filter out salts and impurities, making it a preferred method due to its efficiency and lower energy consumption. Multi-effect distillation employs multiple evaporation and condensation stages, using heat to separate water from salts, suitable for large-scale operations. Other methods, such as electrodialysis and solar desalination, offer alternative approaches to treating saline water, catering to diverse regional needs and resources.</p></p>

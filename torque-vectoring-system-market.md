<p><p>The torque vectoring system market can be categorized into three main types: Front Wheel Drive (FWD), Rear Wheel Drive (RWD), and All Wheel Drive/Four Wheel Drive (AWD/4WD). FWD systems distribute torque to the front wheels, enhancing stability and traction, particularly in compact vehicles. RWD systems allocate power to the rear wheels, offering better handling and performance in sports cars. AWD/4WD systems provide torque to all wheels, improving off-road capability and traction in various conditions, making them popular in SUVs and trucks.</p></p>

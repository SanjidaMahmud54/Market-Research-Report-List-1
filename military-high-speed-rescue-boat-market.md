<p><p>The Military High-speed Rescue Boat market is categorized into two main types: inboard engine and outboard engine boats. Inboard engine boats feature engines installed within the hull, providing enhanced stability and protection from damage, making them suitable for harsh conditions. Conversely, outboard engine boats have engines mounted externally, allowing for easier maintenance and better maneuverability. Both types cater to specific operational needs, with inboard engines typically favored for larger missions and outboard engines preferred for rapid deployment and agility in various rescue scenarios.</p></p>

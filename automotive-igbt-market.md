<p><ul><li>CIB/PIM</li><li>IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive IGBT market consists of various types, primarily focusing on CIB/PIM (Converter-Integrated Battery and Power Interface Module) and IPM (Intelligent Power Module). CIB/PIM integrates power modules for electric vehicle applications, enhancing energy efficiency and compact design. IPM combines IGBTs with driving circuits in a single package, simplifying automotive designs and improving reliability. Both types play crucial roles in electric and hybrid vehicles, contributing to improved performance, efficiency, and thermal management in power electronics systems.</p></p>

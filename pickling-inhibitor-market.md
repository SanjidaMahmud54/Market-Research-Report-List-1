<p><p>The Pickling Inhibitor Market is categorized into two main types: organic acids and inorganic acids. Organic acid inhibitors, such as citric and lactic acid, provide effective corrosion resistance by forming protective layers on metal surfaces during the pickling process. In contrast, inorganic acid inhibitors, like phosphoric and nitric acid, are more potent in controlling oxidation and promoting metal passivation. Both types play a vital role in enhancing metal treatment efficiency while ensuring minimal environmental impact and maximizing operational safety.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Photosensitive Dielectric Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Tone Type</li><li>Negative Tone Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive dielectric material market is divided into two main types: positive tone and negative tone materials. Positive tone type materials become soluble after exposure to light, allowing for the development of intricate patterns, making them ideal for applications like microelectronics and photolithography. In contrast, negative tone type materials become insoluble upon exposure, which facilitates the creation of robust structures by leaving behind the unexposed areas. Both types are essential for various applications in semiconductor manufacturing and advanced packaging technologies.</p></p>

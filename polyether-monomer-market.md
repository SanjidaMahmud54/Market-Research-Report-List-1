<p><p>The polyether monomer market comprises several key types, including MPEG (methoxy poly(ethylene glycol)), TPEG (hydroxyl-terminated poly(ethylene glycol)), APEG (amino-terminated poly(ethylene glycol)), and HPEG (hybrid poly(ethylene glycol)). MPEG is widely used in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics for its solubility and non-toxicity. TPEG serves as a performance enhancer in coatings and adhesives, while APEG offers reactive functionalities for bioconjugation in biomedical applications. HPEG combines characteristics of both PEGs and polyols, catering to diverse industrial process needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The fiberglass body filler market is categorized into two main types: long strand and short strand. Long strand fillers contain longer fiberglass strands, providing enhanced strength and flexibility, making them ideal for larger repairs and structural applications. In contrast, short strand fillers have shorter fiberglass fibers, allowing for smoother finishes and easier sanding, which is suitable for smaller dents and imperfections. Both types serve distinct purposes in automotive and marine industries, catering to varying repair needs and preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>The Barrier CMP Slurry market is segmented into two main types: Copper Barrier Slurry and Tantalum Barrier Slurry. Copper Barrier Slurry is used in chemical mechanical polishing processes to create smooth surfaces on copper interconnects in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing electrical conductivity. Tantalum Barrier Slurry, on the other hand, plays a critical role in depositing tantalum layers that prevent copper diffusion into the dielectric materials, ensuring device integrity. Both types are essential for advanced chip fabrication in the electronics industry.</p></p>

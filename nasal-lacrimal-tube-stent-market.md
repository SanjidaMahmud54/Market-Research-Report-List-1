<p><p>The nasal lacrimal tube stent market offers various types of stents, primarily classified into mono canalicular stents and canalicular stents. Mono canalicular stents are designed for single canaliculus insertion, providing a less invasive option for tear duct dilation and improving drainage for patients with lacrimal obstructions. In contrast, canalicular stents involve two parallel tubes, facilitating drainage from both upper and lower canaliculi. Both types are essential in managing tear drainage issues, enhancing patient comfort and treatment efficacy.</p></p>

<p><p>Non-silicone thermal grease is a type of thermally conductive compound used to enhance the heat transfer between a heat-generating component, such as a semiconductor, and a heat sink or other cooling devices. Unlike traditional silicone-based thermal greases, non-silicone varieties are formulated with alternative materials, often offering better thermal conductivity and performance under extreme temperature conditions.

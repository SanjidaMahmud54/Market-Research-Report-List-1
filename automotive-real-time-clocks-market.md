<p><ul><li>I2C-bus RTCs</li><li>SPI RTCs</li><li>Integrated Quartz Crystal RTCs</li><li>Ultra-Low-Power RTCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive real-time clocks (RTCs) market features several types: </p><p>1. **I2C-bus RTCs** use the I2C communication protocol for efficient data transfer with microcontrollers.</p><p>2. **SPI RTCs** employ the Serial Peripheral Interface for high-speed communication and flexibility in device connections. </p><p>3. **Integrated Quartz Crystal RTCs** combine RTC functions with a quartz crystal for precise timekeeping. </p><p>4. **Ultra-Low-Power RTCs** are designed for energy efficiency, crucial for battery-operated vehicle systems, ensuring prolonged operation while consuming minimal power.</p></p>

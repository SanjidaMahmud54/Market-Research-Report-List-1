<p><p>The antifungal agents market is categorized primarily into three types: Aspergillosis, Dermatophytosis, and Candidiasis. Aspergillosis treatment focuses on combating infections caused by Aspergillus species, often in immunocompromised patients. Dermatophytosis involves fungal infections affecting the skin, hair, and nails, typically requiring topical or systemic antifungals. Candidiasis targets infections caused by Candida species, which can be systemic or localized, impacting both immunocompromised and healthy individuals. Each market segment is driven by varying epidemiological trends, treatment advancements, and patient demographics.</p></p>

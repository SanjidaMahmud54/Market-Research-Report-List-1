<p><p>Aircraft Global Positioning Systems (GPS) are advanced navigation systems that provide real-time positioning, velocity, and timing information to aircraft. Aircraft GPS integrates satellite signals with onboard avionics to deliver accurate location data, supporting functions such as route planning, autopilot, and collision avoidance.</p><p>The Aircraft Global Positioning Systems Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for advanced navigation solutions in both commercial and military aviation. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 11.9% during the forecast period. As the aviation industry continues to evolve, the demand for advanced GPS solutions is expected to rise, further driving market expansion.</p></p>

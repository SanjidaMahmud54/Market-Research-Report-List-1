<p><p>Coherent optical communication equipment refers to advanced technology used in high-speed data transmission over optical fiber networks. Additionally, advancements in coherent DSP (Digital Signal Processing) and photonic integration are enhancing the performance and cost-effectiveness of these systems, further fueling market expansion.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the development of higher-capacity coherent transceivers, the integration of AI for network optimization, and the adoption of open optical networking architectures. The shift toward energy-efficient solutions and the deployment of coherent technology in submarine and long-haul networks are also shaping the industry. As data traffic continues to surge globally, coherent optical communication equipment is poised to play a critical role in enabling next-generation communication networks.</p></p>

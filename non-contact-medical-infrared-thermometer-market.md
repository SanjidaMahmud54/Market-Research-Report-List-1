<p><p>The non-contact medical infrared thermometer market is primarily categorized into two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure surface temperature by detecting infrared radiation emitted from the forehead, making them quick, hygienic, and ideal for screening large populations. In contrast, ear type thermometers utilize infrared sensors to gauge temperature from the eardrum, providing accurate readings but requiring proper insertion. Both types are widely used in clinical and home settings for their convenience and efficiency in monitoring body temperature.</p></p>

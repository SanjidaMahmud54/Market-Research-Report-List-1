<p><p>The in vitro diagnostic medical device market comprises various types: Biochemical diagnostics involve analyzing biochemical markers in bodily fluids for disease detection. Immuno diagnostics utilize antigen-antibody interactions to diagnose conditions, often through techniques like ELISA. Molecular diagnostics focus on genetic and molecular analysis, identifying pathogens or genetic disorders through methods such as PCR. Other diagnostic methods encompass diverse technologies, including imaging and point-of-care tests, expanding the range of tools available for accurate disease diagnosis and management.</p></p>

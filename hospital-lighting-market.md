<p><p>The hospital lighting market encompasses several key types, including troffers, surface-mounted lights, and surgical lamps. Troffers are recessed lights typically installed in ceilings, providing uniform illumination in patient areas. Surface-mounted lights are versatile fixtures mounted directly to ceilings or walls, suitable for various applications. Surgical lamps are specialized lighting systems designed for operating rooms, offering high-intensity, adjustable illumination to ensure precision during surgeries. Together, these lighting solutions enhance functionality, safety, and the overall environment in healthcare facilities.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>BET 100-160</li><li>BET 160-210</li><li>BET 210-300</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Agricultural Silica market is categorized based on BET (Brunauer-Emmett-Teller) surface area measurements, which indicate the particle size and surface properties of silica used in agriculture. BET 100-160 refers to fine silica with moderate surface area, enhancing soil moisture retention and nutrient availability. BET 160-210 offers higher surface area, improving its efficacy as a soil amendment. BET 210-300 includes even finer silica, optimizing plant growth and absorption. The "Others" category encompasses specialized silica types with unique applications in agriculture.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Fatigue Machine</li><li>Medium Frequency Fatigue Machine</li><li>High Frequency Fatigue Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fatigue machine market is segmented into three main types based on frequency: low frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency. Low frequency fatigue machines operate at lower stress levels and are typically used for testing materials under slow cyclic loading. Medium frequency machines bridge the gap between low and high frequencies, catering to a variety of materials and testing conditions. High frequency fatigue machines, operating at elevated frequencies, are designed for precise testing of materials under rapid cyclic loads, enabling detailed analysis of material fatigue behavior.</p></p>

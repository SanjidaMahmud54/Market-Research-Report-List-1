<p><p>The tropical plywood market is categorized into three main types based on thickness: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywood below 6mm is often used for applications requiring lightweight and flexible materials, such as crafts and packaging. The 6mm-18mm range is popular for furniture, interior design, and construction, where durability and strength are essential. Plywood above 18mm is typically employed in structural applications, including flooring and heavy-duty furniture, where high resistance and stability are crucial.</p></p>

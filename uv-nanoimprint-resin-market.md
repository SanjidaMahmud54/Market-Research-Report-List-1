<p><strong>The UV Nanoimprint Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Layer</li><li>Sacrificial Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV Nanoimprint Resin market is categorized into two primary types: Permanent Layers and Sacrificial Layers. Permanent Layers are used to produce enduring patterns on substrates, essential for applications in electronics and optics, where durability and precision are critical. In contrast, Sacrificial Layers serve a temporary purpose, providing support during fabrication processes before being removed, allowing for finer shaping and detailing in multilayer devices. Both types play vital roles in advancing nanofabrication technologies across various industries.</p></p>

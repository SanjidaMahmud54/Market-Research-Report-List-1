<p><p>Cable management in the office market encompasses various products designed to organize and protect electrical wiring. Cable trunking consolidates multiple cables into a single unit for easier management. Cable raceways provide enclosed pathways for wires along walls or ceilings. Cable junction boxes serve as connection points for multiple cables, and other types include accessories that enhance organization and safety, ensuring a clutter-free workspace.</p></p>

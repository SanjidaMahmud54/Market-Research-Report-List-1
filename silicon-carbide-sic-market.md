<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) is a semiconductor and abrasive material widely used in various industries. The SiC market primarily consists of two types: Black SiC and Green SiC. Black SiC, produced from silica sand and petroleum coke, offers high hardness and is commonly used in abrasive applications, refractory materials, and as a semiconductor in high-temperature devices. Green SiC, made from high-purity silica, is characterized by its superior purity and mechanical properties, making it ideal for advanced ceramics, electronics, and cutting tools.</p></p>

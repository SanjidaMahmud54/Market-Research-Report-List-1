The market features two main types: Coulomb Force Type, which utilizes electrostatic forces to attract and hold the wafer, ensuring stability, and Johnson-Rahbeck Force Type, which combines electrostatic forces with mechanical contact to achieve enhanced stability and uniformity. Both types cater to specific application requirements, influencing their adoption in various semiconductor fabrication environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The steep incline conveyor market comprises two primary types: steep slope belt conveyors and steep slope chain conveyors. Steep slope belt conveyors utilize a flexible belt system and are designed to transport materials at angles exceeding 30 degrees, minimizing space while maximizing capacity. In contrast, steep slope chain conveyors employ a robust chain mechanism, ideal for heavy loads and harsh environments. Both types are essential in industries like mining, agriculture, and recycling, where efficient vertical material handling is crucial.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Channel Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Channel Steel</li><li>Light Channel Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The channel steel market is primarily categorized into ordinary channel steel and light channel steel. Ordinary channel steel features a standard profile with a wider flange and thicker web, offering strength and structural stability for heavy-duty applications, such as construction and industrial frameworks. In contrast, light channel steel has a narrower flange and thinner web, making it suitable for lighter applications, such as residential construction and furniture manufacturing. Both types cater to diverse needs in various sectors, influencing their demand and pricing.</p></p>

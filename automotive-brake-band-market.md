<p><p>The automotive brake band market consists of two main types: simple brake bands and differential brake bands. Simple brake bands are used in traditional braking systems, applying friction against a drum to slow down or stop vehicle motion. They are straightforward in design and widely used in various applications. Differential brake bands, on the other hand, are designed for more complex systems, enabling precise control and distribution of braking force in vehicles with multi-speed transmissions or complex drivetrain configurations.</p></p>

<p><strong>The LED Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Lit LED Monitor</li><li>Edge Lit LED monitor</li><li>Full Array LED Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED monitor market features three main types: Direct Lit LED monitors, which have LEDs positioned directly behind the LCD panel for enhanced brightness and uniformity; Edge Lit LED monitors, where LEDs are located along the edges of the screen, providing a thinner design but potentially uneven lighting; and Full Array LED monitors, which use multiple LED zones behind the screen, allowing for better contrast and dynamic range through local dimming. Each type caters to different user preferences and applications.</p></p>

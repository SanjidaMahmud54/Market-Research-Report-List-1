<p><p>The marine drone market comprises two primary types: tele-operated and autonomous marine drones. Tele-operated marine drones are remotely controlled by operators, providing real-time monitoring and interaction for tasks like surveillance or inspection. In contrast, autonomous marine drones operate independently using pre-programmed routes or AI-based navigation systems, allowing them to perform tasks such as environmental monitoring or data collection without human intervention. Both types are increasingly used in maritime operations, enhancing efficiency and safety in various maritime applications.</p></p>

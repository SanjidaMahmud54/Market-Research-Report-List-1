<p><strong>The Coating Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Airless</li><li>HVLP</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coating sprayer market is segmented into three main types: Airless, HVLP (High Volume Low Pressure), and other types. Airless sprayers operate without air, utilizing high pressure to atomize paint, making them ideal for large surfaces and thicker materials. HVLP sprayers use a high volume of air at low pressure for a finer finish, minimizing overspray and wastage, suited for detail work. Other types include manual and electric sprayers, each catering to specific needs and applications within the coating industry.</p></p>

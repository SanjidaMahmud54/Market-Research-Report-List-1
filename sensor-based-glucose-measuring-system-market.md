<p><p>The sensor-based glucose measuring system market comprises two primary types: self-monitoring glucose measuring systems and continuous glucose measuring systems. Self-monitoring systems allow individuals to manually check their blood glucose levels using portable devices, providing immediate results for day-to-day management. In contrast, continuous glucose measuring systems use sensors inserted under the skin to automatically track glucose levels throughout the day and night, offering real-time data and trends, enhancing diabetes management and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble</li><li>Water Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The demulsifier market is categorized into two main types: oil-soluble and water-soluble demulsifiers. Oil-soluble demulsifiers are designed to break oil-water emulsions effectively in oil extraction and refining processes, where they enhance phase separation. In contrast, water-soluble demulsifiers are primarily used in applications requiring the separation of water from crude oil or produced water. Both types are crucial in industries such as petrochemicals, wastewater treatment, and food processing, facilitating the efficient removal of unwanted emulsions.</p></p>

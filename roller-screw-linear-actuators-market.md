<p><strong>The Roller Screw Linear Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard</li><li>Inverted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roller screw linear actuators come in two primary market types: standard and inverted. Standard roller screw actuators utilize a traditional design where the screw and nut are aligned linearly, offering high efficiency and load capacities. Inverted roller screw actuators, on the other hand, feature a unique configuration that places the roller screw below the nut, improving space efficiency and lowering the center of gravity. Both types cater to various applications, providing precise movement and enhanced durability in demanding environments.</p></p>

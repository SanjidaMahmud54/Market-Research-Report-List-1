<p><p>The capacitive touchscreen pen market consists of two main types: active capacitive pens and passive capacitive pens. Active capacitive pens contain electronic components that enhance functionality, enabling features like pressure sensitivity and palm rejection, making them ideal for artists and professionals. In contrast, passive capacitive pens lack built-in electronics, offering a simpler design that mimics finger touch but without advanced features. While passive pens are generally more affordable and versatile, active pens provide enhanced performance for specialized tasks.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug-in Relay</li><li>PCB Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Relays market encompasses key types such as plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are easily replaceable components that provide convenient installation and maintenance, making them ideal for electric vehicles requiring quick servicing. On the other hand, PCB relays are soldered directly onto a circuit board, offering compactness and reliability in space-constrained environments. Both relay types are crucial for managing high voltage and current, ensuring efficient power distribution and operational safety in electric vehicles.</p></p>

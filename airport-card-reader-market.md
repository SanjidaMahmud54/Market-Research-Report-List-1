<p><p>The Airport Card Reader market is categorized into three main types: RFID Readers, Barcode Readers, and Others. RFID Readers utilize radio frequency identification technology for quick and contactless scanning, enhancing passenger flow and security at checkpoints. Barcode Readers, on the other hand, read printed barcodes and are commonly used for boarding passes and baggage tags. The "Others" category encompasses alternative reading technologies, such as magnetic stripe readers and biometric systems, which offer diverse functionalities for airport operations and passenger management.</p></p>

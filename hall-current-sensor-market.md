<p><ul><li>Analog Output Hall Sensor</li><li>Digital Output Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hall current sensor market is divided into two main types: analog output Hall sensors and digital output Hall sensors. Analog output Hall sensors provide a continuous voltage proportional to the magnetic field, allowing for real-time current measurement, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control. In contrast, digital output Hall sensors convert the magnetic field into discrete signals, offering simple on/off detection suitable for digital systems. Both types cater to different applications, ranging from industrial automation to automotive sectors.</p></p>

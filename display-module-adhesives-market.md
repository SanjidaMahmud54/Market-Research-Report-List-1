<p><p>The display module adhesives market comprises two main types: UV curable and non-UV curable adhesives. UV curable adhesives are activated by ultraviolet light, providing fast curing times and strong bonding for various display applications, including smartphones and monitors. Non-UV curable adhesives, on the other hand, cure through heat or humidity, offering versatility in environments where UV light is impractical. Both types are crucial for ensuring durability, transparency, and optimal performance in modern display technologies.</p></p>

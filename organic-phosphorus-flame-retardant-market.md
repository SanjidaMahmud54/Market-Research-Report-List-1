<p><p>The organic phosphorus flame retardant market is categorized into two main types: reaction-type and additive-type. Reaction-type flame retardants chemically bond with the polymer during manufacturing, ensuring long-lasting protection and heat stability. In contrast, additive-type flame retardants are mixed into the final product and can be easily replaced or removed, offering flexibility and ease of application. Both types serve to enhance fire resistance in various materials, catering to diverse industry needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>

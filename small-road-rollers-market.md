<p><ul><li>Single Drum Road Roller</li><li>Double Drum Road Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small road rollers market comprises two main types: single drum road rollers and double drum road rollers. Single drum road rollers feature one large roller in the front, ideal for compacting granular materials and asphalt surfaces, often used in tight spaces. Conversely, double drum road rollers have rollers at both the front and rear, offering enhanced stability and uniform compaction, making them suitable for various applications, including road construction and maintenance. Both types are essential in ensuring smooth, durable surfaces.</p></p>

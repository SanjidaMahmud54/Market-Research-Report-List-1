<p><ul><li>Inert Ceramic Balls</li><li>Active Ceramic Balls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milled ceramic ball market includes two main types: inert ceramic balls and active ceramic balls. Inert ceramic balls are chemically stable and used primarily for applications requiring minimal interaction with other substances, such as packing materials in reactors. Active ceramic balls, on the other hand, possess catalytic properties and can engage in chemical reactions, making them suitable for processes like adsorption and filtration. Both types serve crucial roles across various industries, enhancing process efficiency and performance.</p></p>

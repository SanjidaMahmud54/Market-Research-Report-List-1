<p><p>The 12V lithium battery market primarily features two types: Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) and Ternary Lithium batteries. LiFePO4 batteries are known for their stability, safety, and long cycle life, making them ideal for applications like solar energy storage and electric vehicles. In contrast, Ternary Lithium batteries, typically composed of lithium nickel cobalt manganese oxide, offer higher energy density and efficiency, making them suitable for high-performance applications such as electric vehicles and portable electronics. Each type caters to different user needs and performance criteria.</p></p>

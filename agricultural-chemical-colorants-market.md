<p><p>The agricultural chemical colorants market primarily includes two types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants that can easily penetrate plant tissues and are often used to enhance the appearance of crops or for tracking applications. In contrast, pigments are insoluble and provide a more stable coloration, commonly used in coatings, fertilizers, and seed treatments. Both types play vital roles in improving product visibility, aesthetic appeal, and functionality in agricultural applications, catering to the needs of farmers and manufacturers alike.</p></p>

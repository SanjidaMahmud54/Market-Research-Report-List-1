<p><strong>The Release Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Releasing Agent</li><li>External Release Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Release agents are crucial in various manufacturing processes to facilitate the smooth separation of products from molds or surfaces. The internal releasing agent market involves substances integrated within the production materials, enhancing mold release properties without impacting performance. Conversely, the external release agent market comprises coatings and sprays applied to surfaces to ease product removal post-production. Both markets cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and food processing, optimizing efficiency and improving product quality by reducing adhesion between surfaces.</p></p>

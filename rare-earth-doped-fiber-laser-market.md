CW Fiber Lasers offer a constant output, delivering high power and efficiency, making them suitable for applications like cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit energy in short bursts, allowing for precise control over the energy output, ideal for delicate applications such as medical procedures and material processing. Both types leverage rare earth elements to enhance performance and broaden application possibilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric scooter lift and carrier market primarily encompasses two types: interior and exterior. Interior lifts are designed for vehicle cab interiors, allowing users to safely transport scooters within the vehicle, which helps maintain aesthetics and protection from weather. Exterior carriers, on the other hand, are mounted on the rear or hitch of vehicles, providing an easy-loading space for scooters, facilitating quick access and maximizing interior space. Both options cater to different user preferences and vehicle types.</p></p>

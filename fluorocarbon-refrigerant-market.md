<p><p>The fluorocarbon refrigerant market is categorized into three main types: Hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs), which contain chlorine and are being phased out due to ozone depletion potential; Hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs), which are chlorine-free but still contribute to global warming; and Hydrofluoro Olesfins (HFOs), which are newer, low-GWP alternatives designed to minimize environmental impact. HFOs offer excellent thermodynamic properties while being more climate-friendly, driving a shift in the refrigeration and air conditioning sectors towards sustainable solutions.</p></p>

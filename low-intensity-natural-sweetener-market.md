<p><p>The low intensity natural sweetener market comprises two main types: nutritive sweeteners and non-nutritive sweeteners. Nutritive sweeteners, such as honey and agave syrup, provide calories along with sweetness and are often derived from natural sources. In contrast, non-nutritive sweeteners, like stevia and monk fruit extract, offer sweetness without significant calories, making them appealing for low-calorie or sugar-free products. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking alternatives to traditional sugars while maintaining sweetness in their diets.</p></p>

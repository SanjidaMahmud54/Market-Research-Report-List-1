<p><strong>The Composite Polyester Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biaxial Tension Type</li><li>Uniaxial Tension Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite polyester film market is primarily divided into two types based on the tension applied during production: biaxial tension type and uniaxial tension type. Biaxial tension films are stretched in both machine and transverse directions, resulting in high strength and barrier properties, making them suitable for packaging applications. In contrast, uniaxial tension films are stretched in a single direction, offering specific properties tailored for applications like insulation and protective packaging. Both types cater to distinct market needs in various industries.</p></p>

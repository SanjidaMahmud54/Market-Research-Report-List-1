<p><p>The Embedded Optical Fiber Polarizer market is categorized into two main types: SM (Single-Mode) and PM (Polarization-Maintaining) fibers. SM types are designed for applications requiring efficient light transmission with minimal scattering, making them ideal for standard telecommunications. In contrast, PM types maintain a specific polarization state, crucial for applications in quantum optics and advanced communications where polarization information is vital. Each type serves distinct market needs, influencing their demand in various high-tech sectors.</p></p>

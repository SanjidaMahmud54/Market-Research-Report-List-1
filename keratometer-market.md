In contrast, automatic keratometers utilize advanced technology for quick, precise measurements, often integrating with other diagnostic tools. These automated devices enhance efficiency and accuracy, making them favorable in larger ophthalmic settings and hospitals, driving innovation and better patient outcomes in corneal assessment.</p></p>

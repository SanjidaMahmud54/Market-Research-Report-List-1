<p><strong>The MDI Prepolymers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MDI ( Polyether)</li><li>MDI ( Polyester)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MDI prepolymers market is primarily divided into two types: MDI (Polyether) and MDI (Polyester). MDI (Polyether) prepolymers are used for the production of flexible foams, coatings, and adhesives, offering excellent elongation and versatility. In contrast, MDI (Polyester) prepolymers are preferred in applications requiring greater rigidity and thermal stability, such as rigid foams and automotive components. Both types serve various industries, including construction, automotive, and furniture, catering to specific performance and durability needs.</p></p>

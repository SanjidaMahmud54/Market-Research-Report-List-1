<p><p>The tumor embolization market comprises several techniques utilized to treat various cancers. Trans-Arterial Embolization (TAE) involves blocking blood flow to tumors by injecting embolic agents into the arteries. Drug-Eluting Bead Chemoembolization (DEB-TACE) combines chemotherapy with TAE, using special beads to deliver drugs directly to tumors. Radioembolization (RE) delivers radioactive microspheres to tumors, providing targeted radiation therapy. Additionally, the "Others" segment includes emerging or less common methods that complement these mainstream techniques for tumor control and treatment.</p></p>

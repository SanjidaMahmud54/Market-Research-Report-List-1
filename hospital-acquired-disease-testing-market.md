Bloodstream infections testing detects pathogens in the bloodstream, crucial for timely treatment. MRSA testing screens for methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus, a prevalent antibiotic-resistant infection, highlighting the need for effective infection control measures in healthcare settings.</p></p>

<p><p>The plastic printing ink market is primarily divided into two main types: plastic gravure printing ink and water-based plastic printing ink. Plastic gravure printing ink is commonly used for high-quality, detailed prints on flexible packaging and labels, utilizing a gravure printing process that allows for vibrant colors and intricate designs. Conversely, water-based plastic printing ink is more environmentally friendly, offering reduced volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and optimizing safety. It is widely employed in applications where sustainability and safety are prioritized in printing processes.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive glass market for light commercial vehicles includes three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, commonly used in side and rear windows. Laminated glass consists of a sandwich of glass and plastic, providing superior impact resistance and noise reduction, ideal for windshields. The 'others' category encompasses specialized glasses like tinted or acoustic glass, catering to varying design and performance requirements in light commercial vehicles, ensuring both safety and comfort.</p></p>

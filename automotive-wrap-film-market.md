<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wrap film market primarily consists of two types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is produced by pouring molten polymer onto a surface, resulting in a thinner, more flexible material that offers vibrant colors and better conformability for complex surfaces. In contrast, calendered film is manufactured by pressing and rolling heated polymer, yielding a thicker, more durable film that is cost-effective but less flexible. Both types are used for vehicle customization and protection, each catering to different application needs.</p></p>

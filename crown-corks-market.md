<p><ul><li>Tin-Free</li><li>Tin-Plated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crown corks, used primarily for sealing beverage bottles, are categorized into two market types: tin-free and tin-plated. Tin-free crown corks are made from substrates coated with materials like lacquer or polymer, offering corrosion resistance without tin. In contrast, tin-plated crown corks utilize steel coated with a thin layer of tin, enhancing durability and providing a protective barrier against rust. Both types cater to different market needs, balancing durability, cost, and environmental impact for manufacturers and consumers alike.</p></p>

<p><p>The Photoflood Lamp market primarily consists of two types: flash and continuous light. Flash lamps provide bursts of intense light for brief periods, ideal for freezing motion in photography and generating high-quality imagery. Continuous light, on the other hand, emits a constant light source, making it easier for photographers to visualize the lighting effects in real-time. Each type serves distinct needs, with flash lamps often favored in studio setups, while continuous lights are popular for video production and live streaming applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The environmentally friendly fume purifier market includes various types designed to effectively reduce emissions. Mechanical separation type uses physical barriers to capture particulates, while catalytic combustion type promotes chemical reactions to neutralize pollutants. Activated carbon adsorption type traps harmful substances on carbon surfaces. Fabric filters capture dust and particles, and wet processing type employs water to scrub away contaminants. Photocatalytic type uses light-activated reactions to decompose pollutants, while electrostatic treatment type employs electrostatic charges to attract and collect particles, enhancing air quality.</p></p>

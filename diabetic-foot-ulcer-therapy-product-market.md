<p><p>The diabetic foot ulcer therapy product market encompasses treatments for neuropathic, ischemic, and neuro-ischemic ulcers. Neuropathic ulcers stem from nerve damage, often leading to loss of sensation and injury. Ischemic ulcers arise from insufficient blood flow, resulting in tissue damage and delayed healing. Neuro-ischemic ulcers combine elements of both, characterized by nerve damage and poor circulation. Each type requires tailored therapeutic approaches, including wound care products, advanced dressings, and technology-driven solutions to promote healing and prevent complications.</p></p>

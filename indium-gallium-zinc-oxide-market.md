<p><p>The Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide (IGZO) market is categorized into two types: Crystalline IGZO and Amorphous IGZO. Crystalline IGZO features a well-defined structure, enabling superior electron mobility, which is ideal for high-performance applications like displays and transistors. Amorphous IGZO, on the other hand, lacks a long-range order, making it more versatile for large-area electronics and flexible displays. Both types are pivotal in advancing electronics, particularly in enhancing the efficiency and capabilities of next-generation devices.</p></p>

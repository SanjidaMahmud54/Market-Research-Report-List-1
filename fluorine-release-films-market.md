<p><p>Fluorine Release Films are categorized into three market types based on thickness: Below 30 µm, 30 µm-60 µm, and Above 60 µm. Films below 30 µm are used for applications requiring high flexibility and minimal weight, such as electronics and medical devices. The 30 µm-60 µm range offers a balance of durability and performance, making them suitable for packaging and industrial applications. Films above 60 µm provide superior strength and thickness, ideal for heavy-duty applications and protective coatings.</p></p>

<p><p>The Underdeck Drainage System market is segmented based on mounting styles, specifically "Top" and "Bottom" joist configurations. The "Top" mounting style involves installation above the joists, allowing water to flow into the drainage system while maintaining the aesthetic of the deck. In contrast, the "Bottom" mounting style fits beneath the joists, capturing water that drips down. Both designs efficiently prevent water accumulation, enhancing deck longevity and usability while providing protection for spaces underneath.</p></p>

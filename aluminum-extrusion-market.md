<p><p>The aluminum extrusion market comprises three main types: mill finished, anodized, and powder-coated. Mill finished products have a raw, unprocessed appearance and are often used in structural applications. Anodized aluminum is chemically treated to enhance surface durability and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for decorative and architectural uses. Powder-coated aluminum is finished with a colored powder that is then cured, providing a tough, attractive surface ideal for aesthetic and protective applications in diverse industries.</p></p>

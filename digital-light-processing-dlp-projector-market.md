<p><p>The Digital Light Processing (DLP) projector market is primarily divided into two categories: 1-chip DLP projectors and 3-chip DLP projectors. </p><p>1-chip DLP projectors utilize a single DLP chip and typically employ a color wheel to produce images. These projectors are compact and cost-effective, making them suitable for home theater and portable applications. </p><p>In contrast, 3-chip DLP projectors feature three separate chips for red, green, and blue colors, delivering superior color accuracy and brightness. They are commonly used in professional settings like cinemas and large venues.</p></p>

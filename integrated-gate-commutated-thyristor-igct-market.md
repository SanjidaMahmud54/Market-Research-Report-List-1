<p><p>Integrated Gate Commutated Thyristors (IGCTs) are advanced semiconductor devices used in high-power applications, facilitating efficient control over electric power. The market for IGCTs can be segmented into three types: </p><p>1. **Asymmetric IGCT**: Designed for unidirectional operation, suitable for applications where current flows in one direction.</p><p>2. **Reverse Blocking IGCT**: Allows current to flow in both directions, enhancing versatility in circuit designs.</p><p>3. **Reverse Conducting IGCT**: Combines diode functionality within the device, simplifying design and improving efficiency in power conversion systems. Each type serves distinct applications in energy systems and industrial drives.</p></p>

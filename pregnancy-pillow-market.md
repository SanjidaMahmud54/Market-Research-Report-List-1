<p><p>The pregnancy pillow market is categorized into three main types: U-shaped, C-shaped, and J-shaped pillows. U-shaped pillows provide comprehensive support for the back, belly, and legs, making them ideal for side sleepers. C-shaped pillows are designed to support the back and neck while accommodating the belly, facilitating a comfortable sleeping position. J-shaped pillows offer targeted support for the back and are versatile for various sleeping positions. Each type addresses specific comfort needs during pregnancy, enhancing sleep quality.</p></p>

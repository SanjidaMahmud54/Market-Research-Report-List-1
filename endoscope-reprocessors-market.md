<p><p>The endoscope reprocessors market is segmented into single chamber and multi-chamber systems. Single chamber reprocessors offer a compact design, allowing for efficient cleaning and disinfection of flexible endoscopes in one chamber. In contrast, multi-chamber systems provide a more comprehensive approach, featuring separate chambers for washing, rinsing, and disinfecting, which enhances workflow efficiency and reduces the risk of cross-contamination. Both types are essential for ensuring the safe and effective reprocessing of endoscopes in healthcare facilities.</p></p>

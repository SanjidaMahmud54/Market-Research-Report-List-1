<p><p>The cheese production line market can be categorized into automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic systems utilize advanced technology and robotics to handle processes like curdling, molding, and packaging with minimal human intervention, resulting in high efficiency and consistency. In contrast, semi-automatic systems combine manual and machine-operated tasks, offering flexibility and lower costs while still maintaining a level of automation. Both types serve different production scales, with automatic systems typically catering to larger manufacturers and semi-automatic options suited for smaller operations.</p></p>

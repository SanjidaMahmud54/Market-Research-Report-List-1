<p><strong>The Fiber-digested Silage Inoculants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homofermentative</li><li>Heterofermentative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber-digested silage inoculants enhance fermentation, improving feed preservation and digestibility. The market includes two main types: **Homofermentative** and **Heterofermentative** inoculants. **Homofermentative** types primarily produce lactic acid, rapidly lowering pH to preserve silage efficiently, ideal for high-quality forage. **Heterofermentative** types produce lactic acid alongside acetic acid and ethanol, enhancing aerobic stability and reducing spoilage during feed-out, suitable for challenging conditions. Both types optimize fiber breakdown, but their applications differ based on fermentation goals and silage management needs.</p></p>

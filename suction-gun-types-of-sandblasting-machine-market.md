<p><p>Suction guns in sandblasting machines are divided into dry and wet types, each serving distinct purposes. Dry sandblasting uses a suction gun that blasts abrasive materials in a dry state, ideal for rapid removal of coatings or surface preparation. In contrast, wet sandblasting combines water with the abrasive media, reducing dust and enhancing control over the process, which is suitable for delicate surfaces. Both types cater to various applications in industries like manufacturing, construction, and restoration.</p></p>

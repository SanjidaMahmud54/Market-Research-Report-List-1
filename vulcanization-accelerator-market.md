<p><p>The vulcanization accelerator market is categorized into several types, each serving unique roles in rubber processing. MBT (Mercaptobenzothiazole) is commonly used for natural and synthetic rubber. MBTS (Diphenylguanidine) enhances sulfur curing efficiency. CBS (N-Cyclohexylbenzothiazole-2-sulfenamide) is noted for its fast curing properties. TBBS (N-t-butyl-2-benzothiazole sulfenamide) offers excellent processing safety. MBS (N-Morpholinosulfenyl) provides improved heat resistance. Other accelerators include various specialty chemicals tailored for specific rubber properties, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>

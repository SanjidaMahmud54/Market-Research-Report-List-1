<p><ul><li>Reflective Gratings</li><li>Transmission Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical gratings market is primarily divided into two types: reflective gratings and transmission gratings. Reflective gratings utilize a reflective surface to diffract light, making them ideal for applications that demand high efficiency and minimal optical loss. Conversely, transmission gratings operate by allowing light to pass through them, suitable for applications where space is limited. Both types play crucial roles in spectrometry, telecommunications, and laser systems, contributing to advancements in various optical technologies and applications.</p></p>

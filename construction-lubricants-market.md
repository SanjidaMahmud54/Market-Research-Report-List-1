<p><p>The construction lubricants market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions for machinery and equipment. Hydraulic fluid facilitates the operation of hydraulic systems by transmitting power. Engine oil ensures engine efficiency and protects against wear. Gear oil lubricates gear mechanisms, reducing friction. Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) optimizes transmission performance. Compressor oil helps maintain compressor efficiency and longevity, while grease provides durable lubrication for joints and heavy-load applications, ensuring smooth and reliable operation across construction equipment.</p></p>

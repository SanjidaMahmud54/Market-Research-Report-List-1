<p><strong>The Tunable Light Sources Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QTH</li><li>Xe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tunable light sources are categorized primarily into two types based on their technology: Quartz Tungsten-Halogen (QTH) and Xenon (Xe) light sources. QTH lamps provide continuous spectral output and are ideal for applications requiring stable, variable wavelengths, often used in spectroscopy and microscopy. On the other hand, Xenon light sources offer high brightness and a broad spectrum, making them suitable for demanding photonic applications, including multi-spectral imaging and certain analytical techniques, where rapid switching and high intensity are essential.</p></p>

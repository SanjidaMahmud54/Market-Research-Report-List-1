<p><p>The Conical Mechanical Seals market is primarily divided into two types: Pusher Type Seals and Non-pusher Seals. Pusher Type Seals utilize a dynamic sealing element that adapts to wear and creates a reliable seal over time, making them ideal for applications with variable conditions. In contrast, Non-pusher Seals maintain a consistent seal without any moving parts to adjust for wear, offering durability in stable environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, ensuring effective fluid containment.</p></p>

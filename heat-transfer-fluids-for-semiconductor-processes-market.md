<p><p>Heat transfer fluids (HTFs) for semiconductor processes are crucial for maintaining optimal temperatures during manufacturing. PFAS (per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances) and PFPE (perfluoropolyether) are two primary types used in the industry. PFAS offer excellent thermal stability and low surface tension, making them effective in high-performance cooling applications. In contrast, PFPE fluids are known for their thermal properties and chemical inertness, enhancing reliability in semiconductor fabrication. Both types play vital roles in ensuring efficiency and precision in temperature-sensitive processes.</p></p>

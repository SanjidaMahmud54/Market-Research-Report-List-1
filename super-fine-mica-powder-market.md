<p><p>The super fine mica powder market comprises primarily two types: muscovite powder and biotite powder. Muscovite powder is valued for its excellent electrical insulation, reflective properties, and smooth texture, making it ideal for applications in cosmetics and plastics. In contrast, biotite powder, rich in iron and magnesium, offers unique thermal and mechanical properties, making it suitable for construction materials and coatings. Both types cater to diverse industries, driving growth and innovation in the mica powder sector.</p></p>

<p><p>The whey protein hydrolysates market is primarily categorized into two types: enzymatic hydrolysis and natural hydrolysis. Enzymatic hydrolysis involves the use of specific enzymes to break down whey protein into smaller peptides, enhancing absorption and functionality, ideal for sports nutrition and medical applications. In contrast, natural hydrolysis relies on mild heat and acid treatment, resulting in a more traditional processing method. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences, focusing on factors like digestibility, taste, and physiological benefits.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Hypothermic Machine Perfusion</li><li>Normothermic Machine Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Perfusion Technology market primarily consists of two types: hypothermic machine perfusion and normothermic machine perfusion. Hypothermic machine perfusion involves cooling organs to slow metabolic processes, enhancing preservation for transplantation. This method typically uses cold solutions to maintain organ viability. In contrast, normothermic machine perfusion maintains organs at physiological temperatures, delivering oxygen and nutrients, which preserves function more effectively. Both technologies aim to improve organ preservation and enhance transplant outcomes, catering to varying medical needs and scenarios.</p></p>

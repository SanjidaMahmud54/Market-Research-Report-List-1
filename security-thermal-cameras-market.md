<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Thermal Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The security thermal cameras market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength sensitivity: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave thermal cameras. Short-wave length thermal cameras capture infrared radiation up to 3 micrometers, ideal for high-temperature applications. Mid-wave length cameras, operating between 3 to 8 micrometers, offer balanced sensitivity suitable for various environmental conditions. Long-wave length thermal cameras detect radiation above 8 micrometers, commonly used for surveillance and perimeter security due to their superior performance in low-light scenarios.</p></p>

<p><p>The Vibration Monitoring and Diagnostics System market is divided into two primary types: online and offline systems. Online Vibration Monitoring involves continuous real-time analysis of equipment vibrations, allowing for immediate detection of anomalies and proactive maintenance. This system is typically integrated into existing machinery for ongoing monitoring. In contrast, Offline Vibration Monitoring involves periodic measurements taken during scheduled maintenance or inspections, providing insights into equipment health but lacking real-time data. Both types are crucial for enhancing equipment reliability and longevity.</p></p>

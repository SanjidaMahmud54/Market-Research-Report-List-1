<p><p>Metal Material Based Additive manufacturing, commonly known as metal 3D printing, involves the layer-by-layer construction of metallic parts using additive processes. Businesses are adopting these technologies to enhance production efficiency, reduce material waste, and enable rapid prototyping. </p><p>Innovation in materials, such as the development of high-performance alloys and composites, is also contributing to market expansion.

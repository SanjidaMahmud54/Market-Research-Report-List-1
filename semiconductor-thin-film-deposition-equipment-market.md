<p><p>The semiconductor thin film deposition equipment market includes three main types: PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition), CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition), and ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) equipment. PVD utilizes physical means to deposit thin films, offering high purity and control over thickness. CVD involves chemical reactions to deposit films, enabling uniform coverage on complex surfaces. ALD provides precise layer-by-layer deposition, ideal for nanoscale applications. Each technology serves specific needs in semiconductor manufacturing, contributing to advancements in electronic device performance and miniaturization.</p></p>

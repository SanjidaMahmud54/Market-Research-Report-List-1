<p><p>The Vehicle ADAS Camera Lens market features two primary types of imaging technologies: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high-quality image capture and excellent low-light performance, making them suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems requiring precision. In contrast, CMOS cameras are more energy-efficient, offer faster processing speeds, and are more cost-effective, making them popular in various applications, including lane departure warnings and parking assistance systems. Both technologies enhance vehicle safety and automation.</p></p>

<p><p>Differential pressure sensor ICs are available in two main market types: surface mount and through-hole. Surface mount devices (SMD) are designed for compact, automated assembly processes, allowing for high-density circuit designs and improved performance. In contrast, through-hole sensors offer enhanced durability and stability, making them suitable for high-stress environments. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, such as space constraints, mechanical strength, and ease of assembly, influencing the overall performance and reliability of pressure measurement systems.</p></p>

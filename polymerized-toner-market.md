<p><p>The polymerized toner market is segmented into positive and passive polymerized toners. Positive polymerized toners are characterized by their ability to produce vivid colors and high-quality prints, making them suitable for high-end printing applications. They contain smaller, uniform particles that enhance toner transfer efficiency. In contrast, passive polymerized toners are designed for standard office printing needs, offering cost-effectiveness and moderate print quality. Both types cater to various consumer requirements, optimizing performance based on specific printing demands.</p></p>

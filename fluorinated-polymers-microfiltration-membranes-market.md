<p><ul><li>Cross Flow</li><li>Direct Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorinated polymers microfiltration membranes market can be categorized into two primary types: cross flow and direct flow membranes. Cross flow membranes operate by allowing the feed solution to flow parallel to the membrane surface, promoting a constant filtration process and reducing fouling. In contrast, direct flow membranes facilitate the feed solution's flow perpendicular to the membrane, resulting in a more straightforward filtration approach. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, fouling resistance, and applicability across various industries.</p></p>

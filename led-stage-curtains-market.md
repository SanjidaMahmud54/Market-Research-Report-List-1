<p><p>The LED stage curtains market features two main types: monolithic and stitching. Monolithic curtains consist of a single, seamless piece, providing a uniform display that enhances visuals and simplifies installation. In contrast, stitching curtains are made up of multiple panels stitched together, offering flexibility in design and size. While monolithic options are ideal for larger installations requiring a sleek appearance, stitched curtains allow for versatility and customization, catering to various stage setups and performance needs.</p></p>

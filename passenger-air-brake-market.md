<p><strong>The Passenger Air Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Disc Brake</li><li>Air Drum Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger air brake market encompasses two main types: air disc brakes and air drum brakes. Air disc brakes utilize a rotor and caliper system, providing superior heat dissipation and consistent braking performance, making them ideal for high-demand applications. In contrast, air drum brakes operate through friction between shoes and a rotating drum, offering reliability and ease of maintenance, typically used in heavier vehicles. Both types contribute to enhanced vehicle safety and efficiency, catering to diverse passenger transport needs.</p></p>

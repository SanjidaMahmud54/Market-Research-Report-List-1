<p><p>The cryorefrigerators market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Gifford-McMahon cryorefrigerators utilize a two-phase expansion process for efficient cooling. Pulse-tube cryorefrigerators employ a dynamic gas flow to achieve low temperatures with minimal moving parts. Stirling cryorefrigerators operate based on Stirling cycle principles, offering reliability and compactness. Joule-Thomson cryorefrigerators leverage the Joule-Thomson effect for cooling, while Brayton cryorefrigerators use a closed-loop gas cycle. Other market options include advanced hybrids and specialized systems catering to niche applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Detachable</li><li>Pushable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embolization coils market is segmented into two main types: detachable and pushable coils. Detachable coils are designed to be released from the delivery catheter, allowing for precise positioning and adjustments during procedures. In contrast, pushable coils are advanced into the target area through the catheter but cannot be repositioned once deployed. Both types are used in minimally invasive procedures to occlude blood vessels or tumors, offering essential benefits such as reduced recovery times and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>

<p><p>The Swiss lathe market is primarily divided into CNC and conventional types. CNC Swiss lathes utilize computer numerical control for precision machining and are ideal for high-volume production, offering enhanced automation and complex part capabilities. In contrast, conventional Swiss lathes are manually operated and primarily used for smaller production runs or simpler tasks, providing operators with greater hands-on control. Both types serve distinct needs in industries such as medical, aerospace, and automotive, catering to various machining requirements.</p></p>

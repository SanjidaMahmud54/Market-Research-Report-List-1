<p><p>The heart-lung (cardiopulmonary) bypass machine market is segmented into single roller pump and double roller pump types. Single roller pumps utilize one roller mechanism to facilitate blood flow, making them simpler and more cost-effective, while double roller pumps employ two rollers for enhanced flow rates and oxygenation efficiency. Both types are crucial in surgical procedures requiring temporary cardiac and pulmonary function support, with the choice between them often depending on specific patient needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>

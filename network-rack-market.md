<p><p>The network rack market can be categorized into two main types: wall-mounted and detached racks. Wall-mounted racks are designed for space efficiency, ideal for smaller environments where floor space is limited, providing a secure and organized area for network equipment. Detached racks, on the other hand, are freestanding structures that allow for greater scalability and flexibility, often used in data centers or larger IT rooms, accommodating various equipment sizes and offering improved airflow and access for maintenance.</p></p>

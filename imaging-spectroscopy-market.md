<p><p>The imaging spectroscopy market consists of various segments based on the infrared spectrum. The Visible/Near-Infrared (VNIR) segment focuses on capturing light in the visible range up to 1,000 nm, useful in agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) ranges from 1,000 to 2,500 nm, often employed in mineral identification and moisture detection. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), spanning 2,500 to 5,000 nm, is utilized in thermal imaging and defense applications. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR), from 8,000 to 15,000 nm, excels in heat detection and surveillance.</p></p>

<p><p>The syringe dispenser market is divided into two main types: manual and electric. Manual syringe dispensers require users to manually draw liquid into the syringe and dispense it, offering simplicity and affordability. They are often used in settings where precision is not critical. In contrast, electric syringe dispensers automate the process, providing consistent dosage and reducing user error. They are ideal for clinical and laboratory environments that demand high accuracy and efficiency, despite usually having a higher cost.</p></p>

<p><p>Rear View Camera Modules are essential for vehicle safety, offering clear visibility behind the vehicle. The market primarily includes CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras provide high-quality images with better light sensitivity, ideal for low-light conditions, but are costlier and consume more power. CMOS cameras are more affordable, energy-efficient, and compact, making them popular for modern vehicles, though they may have slightly lower image quality in challenging lighting. Both types cater to diverse automotive safety needs, balancing performance and cost.</p></p>

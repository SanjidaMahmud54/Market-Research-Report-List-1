<p><p>The PP Woven Sacks market is divided into two main types: laminated and non-laminated sacks. Laminated PP woven sacks feature a layer of plastic film that provides enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and printability, making them ideal for products like grains and fertilizers. In contrast, non-laminated PP woven sacks are more economical, offering basic strength and breathability, suitable for bulk dry goods and agricultural products. Each type serves distinct packaging needs, catering to various industries and applications.</p></p>

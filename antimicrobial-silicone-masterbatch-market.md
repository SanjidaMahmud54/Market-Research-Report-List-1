<p><p>The Antimicrobial Silicone Masterbatch market is primarily segmented into two types: thermoplastic resin and thermosetting resin. Thermoplastic resins are characterized by their ability to be molded and remolded upon heating, making them suitable for a variety of applications, such as packaging and medical devices. In contrast, thermosetting resins undergo a curing process that makes them hard and inflexible after initial shaping, offering superior durability and heat resistance, essential for industrial applications and high-performance products. Both types effectively inhibit microbial growth, enhancing product longevity and safety.</p></p>

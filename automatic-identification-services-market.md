<p><ul><li>Vessel-Based</li><li>Onshore-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Identification Services (AIS) market comprises two primary types: vessel-based and onshore-based. Vessel-based AIS involves onboard systems that allow ships to broadcast their location, speed, and heading to enhance maritime safety and navigation efficiency. Onshore-based AIS, on the other hand, includes shore stations that receive and process these signals, enabling authorities to monitor vessel movements, improve traffic management, and respond to emergencies. Together, these systems enhance situational awareness and promote safer maritime operations.</p></p>

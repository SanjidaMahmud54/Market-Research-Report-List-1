<p><p>The polypropylene woven bags market is primarily categorized into laminated and non-laminated types. Laminated polypropylene woven bags feature a protective outer layer that enhances moisture resistance and durability, making them suitable for containing products like fertilizers, chemicals, and grains. Non-laminated polypropylene woven bags, on the other hand, lack this additional layer, making them lighter and more cost-effective, often used for packaging animal feed, rice, and general retail products. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>

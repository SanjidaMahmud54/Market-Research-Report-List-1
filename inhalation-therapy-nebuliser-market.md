<p><p>Inhalation therapy nebulizers are devices used to administer medication directly into the lungs. The market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pneumatic Nebulizers**: Utilize compressed air or oxygen to aerosolize medications, commonly used due to their affordability and reliability.</p><p>2. **Ultrasonic Nebulizers**: Use high-frequency sound waves to create mist, known for being quiet and efficient but may be limited with certain medications.</p><p>3. **Mesh Nebulizers**: Employ a mesh membrane to produce aerosol, offering portability and quick medication delivery with minimal waste.</p></p>

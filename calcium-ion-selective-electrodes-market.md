<p><p>The calcium ion selective electrodes market encompasses two primary types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a liquid phase that selectively allows calcium ions to pass through, providing high sensitivity and rapid response times. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate a polyvinyl chloride matrix to achieve ion selectivity, offering good stability and ease of fabrication. Both types are crucial for accurate calcium ion measurement in various applications, including environmental monitoring, agriculture, and biomedical analysis.</p></p>

<p><p>The artificial lift system market encompasses various methods used to enhance oil and gas extraction. Rod lift systems utilize a rod string to pump fluids to the surface, while Electric Submersible Pumps (ESPs) use electrical power to lift liquids efficiently. Progressive Cavity Pumps (PCPs) employ a rotating screw mechanism for continuous flow. Plunger lift systems use a plunger to separate gas from liquid, and gas lift systems inject gas to reduce fluid density. Other methods include hydraulic lifts and specialized technologies tailored for specific conditions.</p></p>
